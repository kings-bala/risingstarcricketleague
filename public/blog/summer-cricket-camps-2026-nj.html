Summer Cricket Camps 2026 in New Jersey: Train Like a Pro

Published March 6, 2026 • Rising Star Cricket Academy

Get your child ready for an unforgettable summer of cricket! Rising Star Cricket Academy's summer programs offer intensive training, match play, and fun for all skill levels.

Summer 2026 Cricket Training in Central New Jersey

Summer is the perfect time for young cricketers to level up their game. With school out and long sunny days ahead, Rising Star Cricket Academy offers the ideal setting for your child to immerse themselves in cricket at Plainsboro Community Park.

What Our Summer Program Offers

Intensive Skill Development

Our summer sessions go beyond regular training, offering:

  • Extended practice time for deeper skill development
  • Specialized batting clinics with our former Ranji Trophy player, Coach Sushanth Konda
  • Bowling masterclasses covering pace, spin, and swing bowling
  • Fielding bootcamps to sharpen reflexes and ground fielding
  • Match simulation — full-format games to apply skills under pressure

Expert Coaching Staff

Our ACF Level 2 certified coaches bring professional experience to every session. Led by Sushanth Konda, a former Ranji Trophy player, our coaching team provides the kind of elite-level instruction that's rare to find in youth cricket programs.

All Skill Levels Welcome

Whether your child is picking up a bat for the first time or preparing for competitive tournaments, our summer program accommodates:

  • Beginners: Learn the basics in a fun, supportive environment
  • Intermediate: Refine technique and start competitive play
  • Advanced: Tournament preparation and advanced strategy

Why Choose Rising Star for Summer Cricket?

1. Professional Coaching at Non-Profit Prices

As a 501(c)(3) non-profit, we're committed to affordability. Our summer rates are designed to be accessible to all families.

2. Beautiful Outdoor Facilities

Train at Plainsboro Community Park — one of the best cricket-friendly parks in Middlesex County with ample space for full-size matches.

3. Convenient Schedule

  • Regular training: Monday-Friday, 5:30 PM - 7:30 PM
  • Special summer sessions: Additional morning and weekend sessions available
  • Perfect for working parents — drop off and pick up with confidence

4. Tournament Opportunities

Summer means tournament season! Our students get the chance to:

  • Compete in local and regional cricket tournaments
  • Represent Rising Star Cricket Academy against other academies
  • Experience the thrill of competitive cricket

Summer Cricket: More Than Just a Sport

Our summer program builds more than cricket skills:

  • Friendship and community — kids bond over shared passion
  • Outdoor activity — get kids off screens and into the fresh air
  • Discipline and routine — structured training builds good habits
  • Confidence — watching skills improve builds self-esteem
  • Cultural enrichment — connecting with cricket's global heritage

Serving Families Across New Jersey

Our summer program draws families from throughout Central and Northern New Jersey:

  • Plainsboro & East Windsor
  • Princeton & West Windsor
  • Monroe Township & Cranbury
  • South Brunswick & North Brunswick
  • Edison & Piscataway
  • New Brunswick & Highland Park
  • Hillsborough & Bridgewater
