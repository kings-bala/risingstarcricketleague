Best Cricket Coaching for Kids in Central New Jersey

Published March 6, 2026 • Rising Star Cricket Academy

Looking for quality cricket coaching for your child in Central New Jersey? Here's why Rising Star Cricket Academy stands out as the top choice for youth cricket development in the Plainsboro and East Windsor area.

Why Cricket Is Growing in Central New Jersey

Cricket has exploded in popularity across New Jersey, especially in communities around Plainsboro, East Windsor, Princeton, and the greater Middlesex County area. With a vibrant South Asian community and growing interest in the sport among all backgrounds, parents are increasingly looking for structured, professional cricket coaching for their children.

Introducing Rising Star Cricket Academy

Located at Plainsboro Community Park, Park Dr, East Windsor, NJ 08512, Rising Star Cricket Academy is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to developing the next generation of cricket talent in Central New Jersey.

What Sets Us Apart

ACF Level 2 Certified Coaches
Our coaching staff includes ACF (American Cricket Federation) Level 2 certified coaches, ensuring your child receives instruction that meets national standards. Our head coach, Sushanth Konda, is a former Ranji Trophy player who brings elite-level experience to every training session.

Structured Training Schedule
We offer consistent, year-round training sessions:

  • Monday through Friday: 5:30 PM - 7:30 PM
  • Perfect timing for after-school activities
  • Located at the beautiful Plainsboro Community Park

Affordable, Non-Profit Model
As a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit, our mission is to make cricket accessible to every child. We keep our fees affordable so that no aspiring cricketer is left behind.

Skills Your Child Will Learn

At Rising Star Cricket Academy, our curriculum covers:

  • Batting fundamentals — grip, stance, shot selection, and match awareness
  • Bowling techniques — pace, spin, line and length, and field strategy
  • Fielding excellence — catching, throwing, ground fielding, and positioning
  • Game strategy — understanding match situations, teamwork, and sportsmanship
  • Physical fitness — cricket-specific conditioning, agility, and endurance
